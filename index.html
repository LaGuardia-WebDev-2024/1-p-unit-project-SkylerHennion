All About Pickles

An Overview of Pickles

Let us begin with discussing why pickles are the best. They are sour. They are crunchy. They work well in burgers. They can be eaten so many different ways. Whole. Chips. Spears. Diced. Salad. They can be eaten in any occasion. It doesn't just stop at cucumbers. There are pickled beets and pickled mushrooms and the list goes on.

This does not mean that all pickles are equally delectable. Some are actually quite horrendous. Here you will learn which pickles to fiend for and which to keep at least ten feet away from.
